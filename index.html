Aswin Cinemas brought a new concept of not compromising on quality as well as providing outlet for our traditional way of celebrating a movie. The move though a gamble, paid rich dividends as it won the acceptance of audience and the brand value is soaring high with people choosing Rohini as the number one screen in India for watching top hero movies. Fans and audience travel from all over the world to celebrate iconic first day first shows at Aswin Cinemas, especially in the signature Aswin Cinemas Main Screen. Owing to the success Brand Aswin Cinemas has been taken to locations outside of its home base in Chennai to Avadi to Tindivanam and Coimbatore.
